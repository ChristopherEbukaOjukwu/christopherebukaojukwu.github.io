About Me

I'm a 4th-year PhD student in the Computer Science department at the University of Colorado Boulder. My research lies at the intersection of science of science, computational health, and genomics.

Specifically, I develop large-scale data engineering and analytic frameworks to track how disease-associated genes and populations are studied across the biomedical pipeline: from gene discovery to drug development and patient care.

I am a member of the Science of Science and Computational Discovery Lab , advised by Prof. Daniel Acuña.
